Mental Health Isn't An Extra

Some days it's stress or burnout.

Other times it's anxiety, depression, or something deeper.

Whatever it looks like for you, your Curative plan has you covered.

From therapy and psychiatry to recovery support — we've built care that meets you where you are, with $0 cost for in-network options and flexible ways to find the right fit.

  • Medication coverage you can count on
    Get $0 preferred meds for treatment and management prescribed by your provider. Work with your provider to search for preferred medications.
