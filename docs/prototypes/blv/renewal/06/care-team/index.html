REMINDER: How to Find a Provider

Finding the right PCP or specialist is simple with Provider Search, your digital tool for browsing Curative's $0 network.

  1. 1

    Go to Provider Search.
    You'll see doctors, nurse practitioners, facilities and other types of providers covered at $0.

  2. 2

    Filter by what matters to you.
    Choose by location, specialty, gender, or language.

  3. 3

    Check availability and schedule.
    Call to ask about earliest appointment availability.

Need help? Your Care Navigator can walk you through Provider Search.
