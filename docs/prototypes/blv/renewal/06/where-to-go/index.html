Know Where to Go

Primary Care Provider (PCP)

“I prefer to see someone who knows me.”

A PCP is your best option for regular check-ups, managing ongoing conditions, and non-urgent issues.

Telehealth

“I need care but don't want to leave my home.”

Telehealth is a great option for minor issues like colds, flu, allergies, rashes, UTIs, and earaches.

Urgent Care Center

“I can't wait for my doctor, but it's not an emergency.”

Urgent care is designed for urgent but non-life-threatening issues like sprains, minor cuts, and common illnesses like the flu.

Emergency Center

“I need care now”

Go to an emergency center for life-threatening emergencies like sudden severe headaches, chest pain, fractures, seizures, and strokes

Choosing the right place keeps care fast, coordinated, and affordable for everyone.

You're doing the right thing by planning ahead.

By knowing where to go before you need care, you'll save time, reduce stress, and make the most of your $0 coverage.
