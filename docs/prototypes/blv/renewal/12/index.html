Procedures and Surgeries

Let's plan for any upcoming procedures or surgeries you may need in the coming year. What you share here helps your Curative clinician prepare for Part 2 of your Baseline Visit.

Do you have any procedures coming up?

Before Any Procedure, Double-Check:

  • Network status

    Make sure your provider and facility are in-network or covered by the Curative Cash Card

  • Medications

    Some procedures require stopping or starting medications. The clinician performing the procedure can help confirm coverage and alternatives.

  • Prior authorization

    Your provider usually handles this, but it's important to confirm it's been approved.

  • Care coordination

    Loop in your primary care provider so they can guide prep and follow-up.

A Curative Clinician will review anything you select during Part 2 and help you get ready for the year ahead.
