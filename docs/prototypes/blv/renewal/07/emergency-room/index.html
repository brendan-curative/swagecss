Emergency Room

None selected - Care Navigator to assist

The ER is for life-threatening emergencies or when every minute counts.

If you're experiencing chest pain, trouble breathing, heavy bleeding, or signs of stroke, call 911 or go to the nearest ER.

  • Use for:
    Chest pain, seizures, major fractures, stroke symptoms

  • Not for:
    Not for: Cold, flu, minor injuries, or routine prescriptions

How to Find a Facility

It's easy to find covered facilities nearby using Provider Search.

  1. 1

    Go to Provider Search.
    Use your ZIP code or city to find in-network urgent care, ERs, or clinics.

  2. 2

    Filter by type.
    Choose “Urgent Care,” “Telehealth,” or “Hospital/Emergency” to narrow results.

  3. 3

    Save your pick.
    Keep those go-to facilities handy.
