The Curative Cash Card

Your Curative Cash Card is your simple way to access $0 out-of-pocket care.

What's New for 2025

  • Expanded Network:
    Use at any provider in the Curative directory.

  • Nominate Providers:
    You can nominate a new provider for Cash Card use.

  • Smarter Oversight:
    High-cost procedures go through clinical review.

  • Mental Health Flexibility:
    Use the card to pay cash rates at approved, out-of-network therapists.

Quick Reminders

  • Confirm Eligibility: Check the Provider Directory before scheduling.

  • Check Benefits: Only use for services covered in your benefits booklet.

  • Prior Authorization: Some high-cost services require pre-approval.
