Know where to go

Let's make sure you know where to go when you need care fast.

Curative Telehealth

Use for:

Mild illnesses, rashes, medication questions

Not for:

Emergencies or conditions needing physical exams

Urgent Care

Use for:

Sprains, stitches, ear infections, or high fevers

Not for:

Chest pain, severe bleeding, or head injuries

Emergency Room

Use for:

Chest pain, seizures,
major fractures, stroke symptoms

Not for:

Cold, flu, minor injuries, or routine prescriptions
