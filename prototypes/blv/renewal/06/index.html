Know where to go

Let's make sure you know where to go when you need care fast.

Curative Telehealth is for:

Mild illnesses, rashes, medication questions

Curative Telehealth is not for:

Emergencies, life-threatening situations or conditions needing a physical exam

Urgent Care is for:

Sprains, stitches, ear infections, or high fevers

Urgent Care is not for:

Chest pain, severe bleeding, or head injuries

Emergency Room is for:

Chest pain, seizures, major fractures, stroke symptoms

Emergency Room is not for:

CCold, flu, minor injuries, or routine prescriptions
