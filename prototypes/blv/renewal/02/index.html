What to Expect

Keep Your $0 Coverage Active

Your Annual Baseline is your yearly check-in to keep your $0 out-of-pocket coverage going strong.

There are two parts to be considered complete:

1: Curative Refresh

Do this now. Takes 10-15 minutes.

  • A familiar card with a new name. The Curative Cash Card—formerly the Zero Card—still gives you an easy way to access covered services at $0 out of pocket.
  • Expanded network flexibility. Use your card at any provider listed in the Curative directory for eligible services, or nominate a new provider to be reviewed for Cash Card use.
  • Smarter oversight for big-ticket care. High-cost or complex procedures automatically go through our clinical review to confirm the right site of care and keep your plan affordable.
  • More support for mental health. Many therapists don't take insurance—but with the Cash Card, you can easily pay cash at approved mental-health providers and still enjoy $0 cost when covered.

Annual Clinical Check-In

Schedule after you finish the Curative Refresh.

  • Meet with a Curative clinician
  • Update labs or screenings
  • Discuss your care needs

Complete both within 120 days of your plan renewal date to be considered complete and continue with $0 out-of-pocket costs.

Your Privacy Matters

  • Your answers are completely confidential.
  • They won't affect your coverage or rates, and they're never shared with your employer.
  • Everything you share is encrypted and HIPAA-secure.
  • You can update or change your responses anytime.
