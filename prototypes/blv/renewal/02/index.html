What to Expect

Keep Your $0 Coverage Active

Your Annual Baseline is your yearly check-in to keep your $0 out-of-pocket coverage going strong.

There are two parts to be considered complete:

1. Curative Refresh

Do this now. Takes 10-15 minutes.

  • Learn what's new

  • Reflect and confirm

  • Share goals for next year

2. Annual Clinical Check-In

Schedule after you finish the Curative Refresh.

  • Meet with a Curative clinician

  • Update labs or screenings

  • Discuss your care needs

Complete both within 120 days of your plan renewal date to be considered complete and continue with $0 out-of-pocket costs. Your progress saves automatically, so you can step away and return as needed.

Your Privacy Matters

Your answers are completely confidential, never shared with your employer, and won't impact your coverage or rates.
