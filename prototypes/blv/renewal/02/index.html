What to Expect

Your Baseline Visit is your yearly check-in to keep your $0 out-of-pocket coverage.

There are two parts to be considered complete, and you need to compete both:

1. Baseline Visit update

You're doing this now! Takes 10-15 minutes.

  • Learn what's new

  • Reflect and confirm

  • Share goals for the upcoming year

2. Clinical Check-In

You'll schedule this after you finish Part 1.

  • Meet with a Curative clinician

  • Discuss your care needs

Complete both within 120 days of your plan and you'll continue with $0 out-of-pocket costs. Your progress saves automatically, so you can step away and return as needed.

Your Privacy Matters

Your answers are completely confidential, never shared with your employer, and won't impact your coverage or rates.
