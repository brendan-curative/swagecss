What to Expect

Your Annual Baseline helps make sure your plan and care stay perfectly aligned and keeps your $0 coverage active.

There are two quick parts to complete this year:

Part 1: Curative Refresh

(You're doing this now!)

  1. 1

    Check In — Review your care team, preferred facilities, and any medications.

  2. 2

    What's New —

  3. 3

    Year in Review — Reflect on how you used your plan and what could be easier.

  4. 4

    Look Ahead — Share your upcoming care, goals, or procedures for 2025.

** Your progress saves automatically — you can take a break anytime and pick up right where you left off.

Part 2: Annual Clinical Check-In

(Schedule Next)

Once you finish this online refresh, you'll book your Annual Clinical Check-In — the clinical visit portion of your Baseline Visit.

During that visit, you'll meet with a Curative clinician to:

  1. 1

    Review your health history and goals

  2. 2

    Update any screenings or labs

  3. 3

    Discuss preventive and chronic care needs

Both parts together complete your Baseline Visit and keep your $0 out-of-pocket coverage active.

Your Privacy Matters

  • Your answers are completely confidential.

  • They won't affect your coverage or rates, and they're never shared with your employer.

  • Everything you share is encrypted and HIPAA-secure.

  • You can update or change your responses anytime.

What You'll Need

  • A list of current medications (if any)

  • Information on any upcoming procedures

  • About 10–15 minutes of quiet time

  • Don't worry if you don't have everything handy. You can always pause and return later.

Accuracy Helps Us Help You

Accurate, up-to-date information ensures we can coordinate your care properly and keep your coverage running smoothly.
