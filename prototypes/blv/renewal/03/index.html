Legal Agreements & Consents

Please review and acknowledge these important agreements to continue with your annual check-in:

Health Information Sharing Consent

I consent to Curative collecting, using, and sharing my health information as described in the Notice of Privacy Practices to provide care coordination, care management services, and to improve quality of care.

This consent allows us to:

  • Coordinate your care with healthcare providers
  • Share relevant health information for treatment purposes
  • Provide care management and wellness programs
  • Analyze health trends to improve our services

Telehealth Services Consent

I consent to receiving telehealth services, including video consultations and remote monitoring, and understand the benefits and limitations of virtual healthcare delivery.

  • Benefits: Convenient access to care, reduced travel time, and timely consultations.
  • Limitations: Not suitable for emergencies or physical examinations requiring in-person care.
  • Technology Requirements: Stable internet connection and compatible device with camera/microphone.
