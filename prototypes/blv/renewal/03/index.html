The Curative Cash Card

What's New this Year

  • A familiar card with a new name.
    Your Curative Cash Card (formerly the Zero Card) still helps you get $0 care for many services.

  • Easy access to care.
    Check the Curative Provider Search to find providers who take the Cash Card. If you don’t see your provider, you can nominate them for review.

  • Great for mental health.
    A lot of therapists don't take insurance. With your Cash Card, you can pay the cash rate at approved mental-health providers and still pay $0 when covered.

*All Cash Card transactions are reviewed to make sure the service is covered, meets clinical guidelines, and complies with usual & customary standards (including price and prior authorization when required). For limitations on the Curative Cash Card, go to curative.com/cash-card

    You can use your card at in-network and Cash Card-only providers found in the Provider Search.

  • If you're seeking care outside Texas, Florida, or Georgia:
    our Cash Card works best as a backup if your Member ID card isn't accepted.
