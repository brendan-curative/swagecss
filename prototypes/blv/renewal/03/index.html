The Curative Cash Card

What's New for 2025

  • A familiar card with a new name.
    The Curative Cash Card—formerly the Zero Card—still gives you an easy way to access covered services at $0 out of pocket.

  • Expanded network flexibility.
    Use your card at any provider listed in the Curative directory for eligible services, or nominate a new provider to be reviewed for Cash Card use.

  • More support for mental health.
    Many therapists don't take insurance—but with the Cash Card, you can easily pay cash at approved mental-health providers and still enjoy $0 cost when covered.
