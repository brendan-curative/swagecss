The Curative Cash Card

Your Curative Cash Card makes paying for care simple, predictable, and stress-free—so you can focus on your health, not the bill.

Whether you're renewing your plan or using the card for the first time, here's a quick refresh on how it works and what's new.

What's New for 2025

  • A familiar card with a new name.
    The Curative Cash Card—formerly the Zero Card—still gives you an easy way to access covered services at $0 out of pocket.

  • Expanded network flexibility.
    Use your card at any provider listed in the Curative directory for eligible services, or nominate a new provider to be reviewed for Cash Card use.

  • Smarter oversight for big-ticket care.
    High-cost or complex procedures automatically go through our clinical review to confirm the right site of care and keep your plan affordable.

  • More support for mental health.
    Many therapists don't take insurance—but with the Cash Card, you can easily pay cash at approved mental-health providers and still enjoy $0 cost when covered.

How to Get the Most From Your Cash Card

Office visits, imaging, labs, or procedures with in-network providers

Covered mental-health or substance-use services

Prescription or preventive care visits listed in your benefits booklet

Only use your Cash Card for services covered in your benefits booklet.

Some high-cost services may require prior authorization before payment.

Always confirm a provider's eligibility before scheduling care.
